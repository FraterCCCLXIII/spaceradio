# SpaceRadio Sponsorships

Partner model, packages, and agent guidance for sponsor-facing content.

## Partnership thesis

Space projects need cultural reach beyond press releases. SpaceRadio offers:

- **Audience** — space-interested listeners globally
- **Original content** — mission-themed music and shows
- **Transmission** — sponsor-attributed events in the public registry
- **Education** — classroom-ready materials tied to real missions
- **Workforce culture** — internal streams and event audio

## Sponsor categories

| Category | Examples | Primary value |

## Sponsored programming templates

### Named hour

- **Title:** `[Sponsor] presents Artemis Hour` (example)
- **Length:** 60 minutes daily or weekly
- **Contents:** Rotation + 30 sec sponsor sting at top of hour
- **Digital:** Mission page on spaceradio.com with sponsor lockup

### Launch takeover

- **Window:** T-2 hours through T+1 hour
- **Contents:** Custom playlist, live host optional, Tier 1 beam at liftoff
- **Registry:** Public transmission ID linked in sponsor post-mortem

### Education bundle

- 5 lesson plans (grades 6–12)
- 1 sonification or transmission explainer video
- Classroom license for stream embed
- Co-branded "Golden Record 2.0" discussion guide (Phase 5)

## Sponsor portal (Phase 4)

**Features:**

- Listener counts by region (aggregated, privacy-safe)
- Impression estimates for sponsored hours
- Asset download: logos, stings, guidelines
- Transmission history for sponsor-attributed events
- Ticket system for programming requests

**Not in portal:** raw PII, individual listener identities.

## Co-brand guidelines

- Sponsor logo on dark neutral panel; minimum clear space 1× logo height
- SpaceRadio wordmark always visible in sponsor blocks
- No sponsor edit rights over news or editorial voice
- Government logos only with written approval

## Outreach narrative (deck bullets)

1. Space lacks a dedicated cultural audio station — SpaceRadio fills the gap
2. Original AI + human music at scale with provenance
3. Transmission registry offers verifiable, shareable moments
4. Education and museum paths extend sponsor impact
5. Eternity Archive aligns with long-horizon mission thinking

## Agent rules for sponsor content

1. Never name real sponsors unless provided in a brief — use `[Sponsor TBD]`
2. Never claim agency endorsement — use "inspired by" or "covering" for editorial
3. Include disclaimer template from [BRAND.md](BRAND.md) on sponsored pages
4. Link sponsored programming to `Mission` entities in architecture
5. Attribute transmissions in registry with `sponsor_id` when applicable

## Flagship partner criteria

Prospective Flagship partners should have at least one of:

- Orbital or deep-space mission relevance
- Global brand recognition in aerospace
- Museum or education network reach
- Willingness to co-fund Tier 3+ transmission
